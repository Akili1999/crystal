Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random numnber at the beginning of the game There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game by going above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
